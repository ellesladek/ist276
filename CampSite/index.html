General Information

This summer brings four exciting camps to the first three weeks of June! Mercer offers these fun, and exciting camps, designed to promote creativity, while teaching some valuable technical skills. Here are some things you should know:


  1. Each Camp runs for one week from 8:30am until 3:30pm each day, M-F.
  2. Back by popular demand: After Camp Program 3:30pm - 5:15pm each day (additional fee of $35/week).
  3. Camps are designed by college faculty and taught by multiple educators.
  4. Camps meet in Mercer's Computer Science Building.
    NOTE: WE HAVE MOVED INTO A NEW BUILDING SINCE LAST SUMMER.
  5. Camp tuition is $250/camp (cash, check, or money order - or call for credit card option).
  6. Lunch is included and will be served in Willet. If the camper has food allergies, they are required to bring their own lunch.
  7. Campers will receive a Camp T-Shirt (while supplies last).
  8. Recreational breaks will be offered twice each day.
  9. Space is limited for each camp. Minimum enrollment is required to offer each camp.
  10. Check out our "Registration" tab above for links and details general registering for camp.
  11. If you have any questions about our camps, please contact us through our "Contact Us" tab.

 

The Camps

  • 01 Minecraft Coders

    Campers will explore and discover cooperative problem solving skills as they work in a Minecraft virtual world: Creative Crafting, Minecraft Coding, Making things, Management Skills, Team Building.

  • 02 Coding Camp

    Campers will learn common coding languages, giving them the opportunity to create games and apps: Java, C++, Python, and more!

  • 03 Master Robot Builders

    Campers will realize the power and control they possess as they learn to build and program a variety of robots: Building Robots, Coding Robots, STEAM Skills, Battlebots, and Robot Fun!

  • 04 Creating Imaginary Worlds

    Augmented and Virtual Reality are popular technologies for creating imaginary worlds, and both are quickly expanding into our everyday lives. This camp will introduce campers to techniques for navigating and interacting with "imaginary worlds," and expose them to the steps designers use to build them.

    Bringing your Smart Phone is encouraged, not required.

Registration

If you have any questions, or special requests, please contact us. We are always willing to work with you.

How to Apply

Fill out our online application form.

Submitting the Application

When you submit this form, you should receive an email with your complete application packet. If you do not receive the application packet, contact us, and let us know.

Sending the Application

You should then review the application for accuracy, make any necessary corrections, and then mail it, along with your payment (cash, check, or money order), to us.

Payment

You will have two weeks to get your application materials, and payment, to us. If we do not receive your completed application and payment within two weeks, you will forfeit your spot.
